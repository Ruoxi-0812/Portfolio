    About Me

    Hi! I’m Ruoxi Wang, you can also call me Carol, a sophomore at Northeastern University, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Interaction Design.

    I’m continually amazed by the versatility of computer science and its ability to drive innovation and solve real-world problems by connecting industries, people, and ideas through thoughtful design. For me, problem-solving is about diving into research, exploring creative concepts, and finding design-driven solutions that make an impact. My passion for Design lies in its ability to bridge the gap between technology and user experience. While I prefer to maintain a thoughtful sometimes even careful approach, I’m always excited to take on new challenges, whether through clubs, projects, or collaboration. I value working as part of a team and contributing my skills to create meaningful results, and I’m eager to see where this journey takes me.

    You can contact me if you’re as curious about the world of computer science and design as I am!
